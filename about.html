About KFC

History

KFC was founded by Colonel Harland Sanders, an entrepreneur who began cooking fried chicken admsit the period where the Great Depression took place in the United States. He mastered the art of mixing spices and herbs His recipe has now become so popular and has grown into one of the world's largest fast-food chains.

Mission

Deliver delicious, high-quality chicken meals to customers worldwide. Offer quick and friendly service in the welcoming environment. Keeping a high standard of cleanliness and hygiene in all our outlets. We are committed to providing our customers with the best possible dining experience. We strive to create a welcoming and enjoyable atmosphere for all our customers.

Vision

KFC is committed to giving back to the communities we serve. We actively support local charities and initiatives that make a positive impact on the lives of those in need. Across the globe KFC has contributed by:

  • Donating meals to those in need who may be stuggling financially during difficult times
  • Supporting local food banks and shelters
  • Partnering with charities that provide assistance to vulnerable populations
  • Providing support by encouraging education initiatives
  • Funding programs that promote health and wellness in communities

Our company focuses not only just on the business itself but also on the lives of people in the communities we serve. We believe in making a positive impact so that everyone can enjoy a better quality of life.

Team

Current CEO

Scott Mezvinsky

Scott Mezvinsky recently became CEO of KFC in the year 2025. He is in control of the company's global operations and is responsible for driving growth and profitability.

Former CEO

Sabir Sami

Sabir Sami led as CEO from 2022-2025. His leadership was instrumental in expanding KFC's market presence and driving innovation in the fast-food industry. Before becoming CEO, he was responsible for managing the company's operations in several international markets.

Former CEO

Roger Eaton

Roger Eaton served as CEO of KFC for several years before Sabir Sami. During his tenure, he focused on expanding the brand's presence in international markets and improving operational efficiency. Originally from South Africa, he played a vital role in the company's global expansion.
