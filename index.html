Welcome to KFC

KFC (Kentucky Fried Chicken) is a global fast-food chain known for its signature fried chicken developed by Colonel Sanders and other menu items. The company operates thousands of restaurants worldwide, offering a variety of chicken meals, sides, and beverages. KFC is famous for its secret blend of herbs and spices used in its fried chicken recipe.

Purpose of the Website

The purpose of this website is to provide customers with information about KFC's products, services, and promotions, while also offering a platform for customer engagement and support. It also showcases key web development concepts such as responsive design, user-friendly navigation, search functionality, form validation and interactive JavaScript features so that customers can be provided with an engaging experience.

About This Website

This is a multi-page restuarant website page designed for KFC. The main tool used for the creation of the website was Visual Studio Code. The website was developed using programming languages such as HTML and CSS for an assignment. The main purpose of this assignment is to provide users with information about the KFC company, products, specials, enquiries, and contact pages making use of user-friendly interface. The website consists of multiple web pages which are connected through the aid of a navigation menu, hence user friendly interface, making the website easily accessible for users. Users can make use of this website to check menu items, specials and available promotions. Furthermore customers are able to send thier queries to the support team.

Goal 1: Promote Products and imoprove brand awareness

The website enables for the menu items, specials and new products to be displayed in an appealing manner to encourage the customers to purchase. Adequate information can be provided when considering the prices, meal description and the categories each menu item belongs to. Effective advetisement should encourage for increased customer sales and more customer donations which will in turn benefit both the customers and the organisation. Addessing the history, goals and the drive to provide customers with one of the best food will allow for a strong-positive brand image for the organisation.

Goal 2: Enhanced Customer Service and Engagement

Customers are provided with quick and easy access to feedback forms, FAQs, support, and contact information. The webiste is designed to allow an interactive environment where customers are able to explore a range of products, submit forms and stay updated on the latest changes and promotions. In addition, engaging with the organisations support team assists with better awareness regarding the performance of the website as well as the standard of the food quality and delivery service. The customers concerns and enquiries can be addressed effectively and efficiently.

Goal 3: Improve Customer Service and accessibility

An online platform allows for the customer to have easy access regarding the menus available, location of store, time of closing and online delivery services. This platform allows for efficient customer interaction between the customer and the organisation. One of our aims is to desig a well-functioning, responsive and user friendly website across multiple devices.
